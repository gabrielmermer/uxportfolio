ABOUT

Hey, my name is Gabriel. I’m a UX/UI Designer from Poland, currently studying Creative Computing in Austria. I’m eager to travel, be it professionally or personally, even to the end of the world.
I create solutions that not only look good, but also feel right to the user. With my skill set in both full-stack, and mobile development I produce fully integrated solutions, from idea to completion.
Detail chasing is my obsession and my heart beats faster when I think of Design Thinking. My personal philosophy is evoking emotions, keeping the focus on the essence since simplicity is elegance.
Having spent 10+ years organising Scouting groups and organising summer camps for kids I know how to work in multicultural teams under stress, and how to survive in the woods.
